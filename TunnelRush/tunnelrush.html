Tunnel Rush 2018


Tunnel Rush is a fast-paced dodging unity game inspired by Slope and Death Run 3D.

You are moving in a tunnel full of color at a blazing speed and need to avoid obstacles in different shapes.
It is not easy! You need to be very agile and precise to see the position of the incoming obstacle and quickly shift your position away to avoid it. Stay focused and break your record!

Controls
  • A (left), D (right) to move.
  • Space - pause or replay
